Potato and Black Bean Enchiladas

Description

Potato and Black Bean Enchiladas with Creamy Queso Rojo and Pico de Gallo

Ingredients:

  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 TBSP. All-Purpose or Whole Wheat Flour
  • 12 oz. Potatoes
  • 12 oz. can Black Beans(drained)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 bunch Scallions
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 cup (500ml) water
  • 1 Medium-small Tomato
  • 1 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1 oz Achiote Paste
  • 2 oz. shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 6x 6-inch Flour Tortillas
  • Cooking Oil

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Pre-heat Oven and Prepare Potatoes:
    Pre-heat oven to 450℉ (232℃). Wash and dry produce. Peel and dice potatoes. Place in a medium pot with enough salted water to cover by 2 in (5 cm). Bring to a boil ans cook until tender, 5-7 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make Pico de Gallo:
    Meanwhile, finely dice tomato. Trim and thinly slice scallions seperating the whites from the greens. Finely chop garlic. Half lime; squeeze juice from one half into small bowl. Cut remaining lime into wedges. To bowl with lime juice, add tomato, 1 TBSP scallion greens, and 1 TBSP oil. Stir to combine; season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Prepare Queso Rojo
    In pot used to boil potatoes, melt 2 TBSP butter over medium heat. Whisk in half of the achiote paste and 2 TBSP Flour; cook, whisking constantly, until flour is fully incorporated, 30-60 seconds. Slowly whisk in 1 cup (250ml) water
  4. Make Filling:
    Once potatos are done, heat a large drizzle of oil in a medium pan over medium-high heat. Add drained potatoes, scallion whites, beans, garlic, remaing achiote paste,1/4 cup of water , and a pinch of salt until combined. Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid has slightly reduced and beans are warmed through,3-4 minutes. Stir in remaining Mexican Cheese Blend until thoroughly combined. Transfer filling to a bowl and set aside to cool.
  5. Building Enchiladas:
    Place a small amount of filling on one half of tortilla. Roll up tortillas, starting with filled sides, to form enchiladas. Place, seam sides down, in a lightly oiled 8x8 in baking dish or ovenproof pan.
  6. Finishing touches:
    Pour Queso Rojo over enchiladas to thoroughly coat. Top with Pico de Gallo, sour cream, and remaining scallion greens. Divide the enchiladas between plates and serve with remaining lime wedges on the side.
